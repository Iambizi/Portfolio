3-D Animations built with the THREE.js library.
Learned how to set up a basic scene and create different primitive geometry shapes, materials, lighting and camera positions to make some fun 3-d animations.
Through the use of orbit controls I was able to add a little bit of interactivity to the Animations; you can zoom, click and drag to position the camera at diffrent angles of the scene.
